## Component usage

Use the component to display statuses, labels, or categories.

```jsx
<Badge label="New" />
```
### Filled Badges
Badges with a solid background color to emphasize their content. Different colors can be used to reflect status or category.
```jsx
<div style={{ display: 'flex', gap: '10px' }}>
  <Badge label="Default" />
  <Badge label="Primary" color="primary" />
  <Badge label="Secondary" color="secondary" />
  <Badge label="Success" color="positive" />
  <Badge label="Error" color="negative" />
</div>
```
### Outlined Badges
Badges with a transparent background and a colored border, useful when a more subtle appearance is desired.
```jsx
<div style={{ display: 'flex', gap: '10px' }}>
  <Badge label="Default" variant="outlined" />
  <Badge label="Primary" variant="outlined" color="primary" />
  <Badge label="Secondary" variant="outlined" color="secondary" />
  <Badge label="Success" variant="outlined" color="positive" />
  <Badge label="Error" variant="outlined" color="negative" />
</div>
```
